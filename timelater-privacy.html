Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Welcome to Time Later, a professional photo stamp and EXIF editor application. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Data Collection and Usage

At DR SAPTO LAB, we value your privacy above all else. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information or photo data on any external servers.

All photo processing, including adding watermarks, stamps, and map data, is performed locally on your device. Your photos never leave your device unless you choose to share them yourself.

2. Permissions

To provide its core functionality, the App requires the following permissions:

  • Location Services: We use your GPS coordinates to automatically determine the location and address to be stamped on your photos. This data is processed locally and is not shared with us or any third party.
  • Camera & Gallery Access: Required to capture new photos or select existing ones for editing.
  • Storage Access: Required to save your edited photos back to your device's gallery.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses OpenStreetMap and Flutter Map to display map snapshots in your watermarks. These services may receive your IP address or coordinates solely to provide the map tiles required for the app's functionality. We do not use any tracking or analytics SDKs.

4. Data Retention

Since we do not collect your data, we do not retain it. Any data created (stamped photos) is stored directly in your device's storage and is managed entirely by you.

5. Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. However, since all our processing is offline/local, the risk of external data breaches is significantly minimized.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at drsapto.labs@gmail.com.
